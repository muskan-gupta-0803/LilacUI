Different Size Avatar

A standard avatar component can be of 5 different sizes i.e. extra small(xs), small(sm), medium(md), large(lg) and extra large(xl)

Text Avatar

A text avatar is the one in which in place of image a normal text representation is shown.

Avatar with badge

Avatar with badges have a small dot like icon at the top or bottom of avatar component.
It has three main states: active(green), inactive(grey) and dnd(red).
